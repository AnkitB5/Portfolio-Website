About Me

Hello! I'm Ankit Bhattarai, a first-year student at Metropolitan State University of Denver pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. My technical proficiency encompasses languages such as Java, JavaScript, CSS, and HTML, complemented by hands-on experience with troubleshooting procedures and network basics. Beyond technical skills, I bring a wealth of interpersonal attributes, including leadership, problem-solving, communication, creativity, and innovation.

Currently, I work for the TRIO program where I serve as a mentor, providing tailored academic support in Math and Computer Science. My prior role as an IT Assistant Technician at LAXMI Group & DVLS involved orchestrating technical support, network security, website maintenance, and data analysis, showcasing a holistic understanding of technology's multifaceted role in business operations.

My proficiency in multiple languages, including English, Nepali, Hindi, and Urdu, aligns with a diverse range of interests. These span programming, community service, nature, and soccer, reflecting my holistic and passionate approach to life and continual learning.
